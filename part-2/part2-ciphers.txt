Simple Substitution Cipher using a keyword
1.Cipher text alphabet starts with 1st keyword with duplicate letters removed and ends with 2nd keyword(i.e. apples -> aples)
2.Rest of alphabet not used from keyword is written out in usual order between the two keywords, with the keywords as "book-ends"
1st keyword = ICARUS
2nd keyword = MONK
